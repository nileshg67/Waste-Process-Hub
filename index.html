Waste Management Process

Collection & Transportation

Waste is efficiently collected using eco-friendly trucks and transported to sorting facilities.

Sorting & Separation

Waste is divided into recyclables, compostables, and hazardous materials for processing.

Recycling

Plastic, paper, glass, and metals are repurposed into new materials, reducing landfill burden.

Composting

Organic waste is decomposed into nutrient-rich compost that supports local farming.

Energy Recovery

Non-recyclable materials are converted into usable energy, reducing carbon footprints.

Safe Disposal

Remaining waste is disposed in engineered landfills with environmental safeguards.
